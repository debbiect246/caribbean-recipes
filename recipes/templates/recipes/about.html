About these recipes...


I love Caribbean food!


I have lived in London all my life and my father was from the Caribbean island of Grenada.

Sadly he passed away in 2008 but I love to collect and cook the food that he used to cook for us.

I have started this collection of Caribbean recipes.

If you are fond of caribbean food or maybe want to give it a try please take a look.
